Guacomole Recipe

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Mayo and Mustard
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cold cuts

Steps

  1. Spread mayo and mustard on both slices of bread
  2. Place lettuce and tomato slices on one slice
  3. Place cold cuts on the other slice
  4. Place one slice of bread on the other
  5. Eat
