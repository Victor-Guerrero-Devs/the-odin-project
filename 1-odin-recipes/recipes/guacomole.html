Guacamole Recipe

Ingredients

  • A bag of tortilla chips
  • 3 avocados
  • Salt and pepper
  • Diced tomatos
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Peel the avocados and slice them
  2. Place them in a bowl along with the diced tomatoes
  3. Mix both until it becomes like paste
  4. Pour some lime juice
  5. Season to youre liking with salt and pepper
  6. Eat with tortilla chips
