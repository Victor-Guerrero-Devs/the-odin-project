Ramen Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 eggs
  • Sliced garlic and scallions
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Ramen noodles

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a pan
  2. Place all ingredients into the boiling water
  3. Stir for 20 minutes
  4. Turn off the heat
  5. Wait for it to cool down and eat
